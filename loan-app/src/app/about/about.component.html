About

Loan_App has been providing loans to the citizens of House Ravenclaw for more than 15 years. The beginnings of Loan_App start back in the year 2000. With the community lacking a local lending company, Helena Ravenclaw saw an opportunity to provide a much-needed service to the citizens of Ravenclaw. Rowena has lived her whole life in Ravenclaw, in fact, the name of the town was named after her Great-Great-Great-Great-Great Grandmother Rowena. At present, Loan_App has four employees to Help their customers in need. Perpetua Fancourt is the loan officer. Myrtle Warren is the bank teller. Olivia Green is the mortgage consultant. Penelope Clearwater is the financial advisor.

Just recently they have added a new loan calculator to their web site. This calculator will allow customers to enter details on a loan such as the loan amount, interest rate, and number of years and the calculator will give you a monthly payment as well as the amount of interest that will be paid over the life of the loan.
