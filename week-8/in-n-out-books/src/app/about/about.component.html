Who is Stephen King?

Stephen King, born September 21, 1947, is an American author of horror novels. Born in Portland, Maine, Stephen King spent his childhood between his mother and father's place. At the age of 11, he moved with his mother to Durham, Maine. His first short story sale was "The Glass Floor" in 1967. In 1974, King's first novel Carrie was published. In his career, Stephen King has published over 60 books and is considered as one of the world's most successful writers. Stephen King currently Stephen lives in Maine and Florida with his wife, novelist Tabitha King.
